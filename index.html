About Tina

My name is Tina Bizaca and I'm passionate about sharing ideas and communicating visually. I believe simple is not stupid and complexity doesn't need to be confusing. I am passionate about good experiences.

I'm a visual communicator

No matter the title, my primary role is always that of a visual communicator. From good visual communication comes clarity of complexity. When large data and intricate concepts can be condensed into big picture summaries, these ideas are transformed into accessible information. Only once the big picture is understood can the details be analyzed.

My goal is to always grasp the overall idea or problem and transfer the knowledge to others easily so a conversation can be encouraged:
